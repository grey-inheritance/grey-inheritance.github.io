# 001 sun day festival
**2026-06-27**

> *The party is arrested as newly designated Grey Hearts, imprisoned overnight, meets the mysterious Orion Preygle, witnesses three public executions during the Sun-Day Festival, and receives permanent Grey Heart brands before being released into a hostile Vallaki.*

20th annual sun-day festival, celebrating the single day that sunlight returned to Barovia. Guards gather those who are blamed for its disappearance.

- we open with [[Zee]] & [[Inyx]] at a bonfire, with zee cooking and inyx practicing her laugh. They see footsteps & a torch light approaching as a guard captain Zee recognizes, [[Captain Estaniel]], accompanied by four men.
- zee greets the men & Inyx covers herself. [[Captain Estaniel]] says "we want no trouble, but your distasteful jokes will not go unnoticed for much longer."
- inyx says she's zee daughter.
- captain estaniel says they've both been selected & will be joining the other grey hearts in the pit tonight (the prison). they agree to go eventually with inyx confusing the pit for a dance, the guard tells them to leave behind anything they don't want confiscated. they're allowed to finish their meal & head off to a prison owned by the watchter clan.
- we zoom out to broken alley & see two stores, a watchter guard platoon approaches malvara's store "[[happily emerald after]]" - captain kramer & his squad. malvara flusters them a lil & they take her tomato plants while she leaves her valuables & they then take her to prison.
- captain kramer approaches [[Sully]] for his lack of devotion to their lord. sully convinces him that his cane & cat are necessary to bring with him & they agree.
- sully & malvara are brought to the prison as well.
- inyx brushes malvara's hair in comfort & performs a beautiful dance for us in the prison.
- we hear a guard say "block b, prisoner entering". the gates open & a sturdy, middle-aged, man walks in. short black hair & a beard. inyx sees a radiating sunlight behind this guy, but he's wearing a scowl. she feels that he's pure and with great intention but not an exact match. his name is [[Orion Preygle]]. 
- orion asks us some questions: "i fear i'm not along for this world. i don't think a simple stoning is what they have planned for me, but you should never lose hope."  "when someone lies for long enough, do you think they start to believe it?"
- sully says yes, due to his wife's illness.
- malvara says she lies to herself all the time, & believes it.
- zee remains silent & orion does not probe.
- inyx says she made of lies that came true. she says when enough believe something, it comes to exist.
- inyx notices malvara wants a prince charming.
- orion says he's tried to leave barovia many times. "do you believe hope is something a person finds?", "would you stay if the fog opened again?"__
- zee says he would stay because no one likes him anywhere he goes
- malvara glances at zee & says she would stay
- sully would stay for the children / betterment of the land
- orion has a woman waiting for him, Eden. simply friends, & someone he looks up to greatly but he wishes they were betrothed.
- inyx asks if they plan on killing orion & his light dims slightly. "i believe i'm going to be executed tomorrow for conspiracy. as you know, there are many people here who wish to see change. there's many of us, but i wasn't so careful this time around. so i believe i'll be hung come morning. i fear helping me would cause more harm than good. i don't fear death, but the thought of leaving my eden behind hurts more than anything i could imagine. i'm sad to leave her but i dont want to disrupt what's in motion."
- zee casts rumor to make the guards believe the captain was calling them upstairs, & inyx attempts to pick the lock for sully to turn him invisible.
- orion: "im putting my full faith in you, if you fail to act upon this hundreds of lives will be in danger."  he hands us a piece of paper, orion leaves & tells us not to let the paper fall into the wrong hands.
- zee casts minor illusion to create a distracting scream, orion seems to make it out of the main hallway but his fate is unknown to us.
- the paper says: *sun-day festival, night fall, blue water inn, the crow flies low*
- broken alley is half burned down & never restored, we know the blue water inn is nestled there
- inyx sends her butterfly out to spy on the guards - she sees a door ajar & assumes it could have been orion getting out or the captain catching on.
- **long rest**.

- the next morning, the sounds of a celebration and the guard + two captains wake us & manacles us in a line.
- we're led to the pits & do not spot orion anywhere nor the same green checkered captain that held the keys to the cell.
- civilians line the street we're being walked down & yell at us within the parade of shame.
- we're led to the town square with a large platform resembling a gallows where the jeering continues.
- a new person arrives as the masses part for them, a woman - [[Fiona Watchter]] (family uprooted the burgomaster & took control of Vallaki. she only comes out once a year on the sun-day festival & appears just as young as she ever did.)  (A VAMPIRE APPARENTLY !!!!)
- Fiona: "hold your anger against the shameless that stand behind me." the music & crowd dies down as she takes the stage. "my lovely citizens, followers, children of the almighty strahd; thank you for joining me for the 20th annual sun-day festival. it is my pleasure & honor to lead such a strong and faithful people. yet, we must remember why we celebrate what might otherwise seem as another ordinary day. you can see there is no sun above but 20 years ago we were blessed with a full day of sunlight. how fleeting it proved. our lord strahd wants nothing more than to part this fog & bestow the light upon us again but there remains a certain kind of people: a collection of fiends who hinder his loving hand. that is why we gather today, to reprimand and educate those who bring this spell to us. those we call grey hearts. today my people, we label them publicly in hopes of changing their ignorant ways. we chip away at the misery they spread so that the light may return to us once again. although everyone behind me will bear the mark of a grey heart , a special few have proved especially heinous this year:  stamen brax, joshua lingmore, coleman well, orion preygle. you stand accused of the most terrible act of conspiring against our lord himself. for this, you are sentenced to death. i am hoping one has already been dealt with, so we have settled for three."
- the men are brought over and a rope is placed around their necks - their crimes re-iterated. "you three have desecrated and spat upon this holy and pure land. should you have any final words you will have to swallow them." they die.
- inyx senses their complete loss of hope via death & feels the memory of a grave digger. he buried a young man whos faced had been destroyed. the mother thanked him but weeks later the son returned alive. he was never able to identify why & she feels he was wrongly accused.
- she feels the other man with a deep memory of reprimanding his daughter for laughing too loud. eventually his daughter stopped laughing entirely - he went his whole life ashamed of taking his daughters joy away completely.
- malvara attempts to steady inyx, sees her eyes are like soulless & tells zee his gf is going thru it. (they're not dating lol)
- inyx tells us she saw the men's final thoughts before being killed & how full of despair they were.
- fiona: "now, everybody, enjoy the sun-day festival & don't forget to let them know their crimes." she walks off the stage & backs through the crowd.
- a few guards come through with glowing blue brands (hearts with horizontal lines through them.)
- inyx & malvara seem to be the only women in line.
- zee accepts the tattoo & malvara is a little reluctant until zee says they'll match. inyx is on autopilot & doesn't respond so she accepts it unreluctantly.
- sully wears leather gloves that match his dress shoes but he takes them off as slowly as possibly to annoy them & sticks his hand out with a smile.
- we see at our feet what people have been throwing at us: some jewelry that's cracked and broken. in front of the stage: a large metal brazier where people burn the clothes they got from sully. they yell obscenities @ inyx like "strahdless being".
