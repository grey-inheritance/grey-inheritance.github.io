Solomon “Sully” Dracovy

I do not believe I've said any such thing.
— Sully, speaking on the things he's definitely thought about Strahd.

Soloman is a Genie Paladin and resident gentleman dad of the quartet. He previously owned a tailor's shop in Broken Alley, Vallaki.

Appearance

Sully is a tall man with a well-kept beard often seen wearing equally distinguised clothing, the most notable of which being his cane and tall, tailor-made tophat. The priary thing distinguishing Soloman from other Barovians are the unmistakenable horns sprouting from his head, revealing an unmistakenable fey lineage others do not have.

Personality

Solomon is a polite, respectable man who comes across courteous and patient. He holds firms in his personal values, however, and refuses to bend to the will of those who don't align with, even if it's to his own detriment.

Known Skills

Soloman is a reknown tailor in Vallaki, and holds enough innate, respectable influence to intimidate those in charge.

In combat, Sully's cane doubles as a strong weapon he's proven to wield with ease in combat. He seems capable of healing himself when needed as well.

Outside of combat, Soloman has also made reference to being reliably persuasive, though the extent of which has yet to be seen.

History

Soloman has lived in Barovia the longest by age alone, at least within the party's memory. He owned a tailoring business next to Malvara's in Vallaki, and got along well with the children in town. The same couldn't be said for other generations, but his craftsmanship made them willing to overlook his pride for a while.
