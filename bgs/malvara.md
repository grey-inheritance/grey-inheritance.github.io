> *"Why, I lie to myself all the time!"* 
> –  When asked if people start to believe the lies they tell themselves long enough

- the Cain family derives a lineage of vampires who cultivated their heritage with pride and viewed themselves as caretakers of old rather than monsters
- the multi-generational household originally resided in a secluded manor surrounded by lush countryside where elders would teach magic, folklore, & tradition
- Malvara grew up on stories & theatre, thus developing a habit of viewing life as if it's a grand narrative
- due to a newfond lack of success for others/the extended Cain family in the vampiric marriage market, Malvara believed she & her family might now be cursed due to her mother falling in love & bearing a child with a human man
- the family hears word of Barovia as a 'paradise held by __the first vampire__', and travels there as a means of introducing Malvara to more of their kind
- they quickly realize Strahd is cruel & selfish, and their plans to settle down are abandoned immediately
- Malvara's magical studies take a backseat as the family focuses on survival & finding a way out
- the sun-day festival provides them all the opportunity to leave, however Malvara refuses to leave due to a budding crush with a Vistani ([[Zee]]) that leaves her clinging to her hopes for a future within Barovia
- her grandmother stays behind with Malvara, but soon remains with the Vistani while Malvara begins a life for herself within Vallaki
- Malvara befriends another vistani circus performer named Venneca, as well as an older Vallakian neighbor named [[Sully]], who help her as best they can with establishing her business in Vallaki
- Malvara's grandmother passes away due to, what Malvara believes, is incredibly old age
- Malvara's feelings for Zee develop further due to his frequent visits made to her jewelry shop/home, & the success of her business grows due to her friends & the Wachter family's necessity
- Venneca expresses to Malvara that she's developed a crush on Zee as well and appears likely to confess it to him first
- Malvara quietly (and immediately) pulls some social strings, landing Venecca a spot within the carnival domain of dread & effectively removing her as a romantic rival
- the plan appears to have accomplished nothing, as Malvara is still single & now under further scrutiny for her associations & how she's used them
- recently Zee has appeared to be involved with someone else now (Inyx), making her actions against Venecca feel pointless & thus reinforcing the idea of a romantic curse
- nevertheless, Malvara maintains the belief that her life is a story simply awaiting it's happy ending — remaining both theatrical & deeply optimistic as she romanticizes her entire life and often reenacts conversations to herself, with a hope that one day they might turn out the way she envisions them
