Inyx

AHAHAHA. Was that too loud? Let me try again. ha ha ha ha.
— Inyx, practicing her laugh.

Inyx is a Divine Soul Sorcerer.

Appearance

Inyx is a fairy-like creature from an unknown realm. She has translucent wings at her back, albeit broken up and seemingly unusuable. She seems able to summon flowers from her form. All of her features sport a similar pale, lavender hue — including the wings and flowers.

Personality

Pure-hearted and naive are the most likely values to attribute to Inyx. She seems to care deeply for the emotional state of others, as shown by her powers to seek and grant out wishes for the hope of others. While new to the land, she's trying her best to try and blend in.

Known Skills

Inyx has mentioned living her life granting wishes for those of "pure heart". She seems well versed in the sort of magic that has made her innately able to do this.

In combat, Inyx has been shown to use ice-binding spells and the memories of other souls to haunt a target.

Outside of combat, Inyx is able to sense the level of hopelessness within a person, as well as occasionally extract negative memories from them.

History

A bright, pure soul is apparently what Inyx was chasing after upon entry to Barovia; a light that she has since been unable to locate. She isn't sure how long she's been here before being woken up by Zee, or why she looks and feels different.
