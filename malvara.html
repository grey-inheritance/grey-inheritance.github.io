Malvara Cain

Why, I lie to myself all the time.
— Malvara, when asked if people start to believe the lies they tell themselves long enough.

Malvara is a Chronurgy Wizard and seemingly under-the-radar dhampir of the lands. She previously owned a local jeweller's in Broken Alley, Vallaki aptly named  Happily Emerald After.

Appearance

Malvara is a moderately tall woman with curly hair and a darker complexion. She's often seen wearing romantic era clothing, such as tight-fitting bodices and off-shoulder sleeves.

Personality

Most know Malvara as either a romantic at heart or an unnerving delusionist. She seems to hold a tender respect for Inyx and comes across soft-spoken. Despite this, she has shown to readily agree with Zee even if it means lying outright, so this general agreeance has unclear motivations.

Known Skills

Malvara is a jeweler who owns her own shop in Vallaki.

In combat, Malvara has been seen casting ominous necromantic spells and time-altering magic.

Outside of combat, Malvara has described being able to fuse her magic into the jewels she creates.

History

Malvara recalls being in Barovia since she was a child. She owned a jeweller's shop in Broken Alley, Vallaki, named  Happily Emerald After  before being branded a Grey Heart. Her suspicious home-theatrics and singular tomato plant seem to be what put her on thin ice.
