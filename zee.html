Zindelo “Zee”, or “Zinzon”

See guys? Told you we'd be fine!
— Zee, after being painfully branded as a Grey Heart alongside his peers.

Zee is a dance bard and local nuisane to Vallaki. He found Inyx in the woods one day and seemed more than eager to have a companion.

Appearance

Unerringly tall with dark features underneath a telling jester suit of black and green. Zee has a white ceramic mask he wears most of the time, and red eye paint that matches the green of his mask underneath.

Capable of turning into a fiendish shadowform at will.

Personality

The main thing most would notice on contact with zee is a certain cheeky, blasé touch to everything he says. It isn't always there, but it's certainly where he defaults. He'll give the longest lineup of white lies to your face with the same expression he gets talking about the weather. He thinks it's just hilarious.

Known Skills

While Zee calls himself a jester, he isn't known to be particuarly funny or amusing, and instead seems to wear the costume as an excuse to disguise himself.

He carries around a crossbow, but has only referenced using it to hunt.

In combat, Zee is able to turn himself into a shadowy form that proves horrifying enough to cause death from fright upon approach. Otherwise he seems to just loom ominously.

History

Although seemingly having lived in Barovia a long time, Zee has made reference to being able to go elsewhere. It's unclear where exactly he's tried to visit before.
